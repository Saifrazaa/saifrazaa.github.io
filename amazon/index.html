When should I start with the publishing process?

Publishing is a time taking process and involves quite a bit of work before your book can be published. It is always best to work with your publisher even when you are in the writing phase.

Do I share my royalties and ownership with the publisher or anyone else?

No, you own all the royalties and 100% ownership of book. However, publishing platforms like Amazon Keeps a certain proportion of your sales.

What is an ISBN and do I need one?

An ISBN is like a fingerprint making it essential for every book. It is the International Standard Book Number unique to your book and its formats. It is an accurate way of searching and identifying books. This is especially helpful when two books (or more) exist with the same name.

How do I submit my manuscript?

You can submit either a physical copy or a digital copy of your manuscript. We can work with multiple formats for your manuscript.
